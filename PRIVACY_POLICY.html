Privacy Policy

NECHTO: Infection Protocol · Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application NECHTO: Infection Protocol ("Application") for desktop and mobile devices, which was created by bigmeco.

1.What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided & Automatically Collected Information: The Application does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, user identifiers, or analytics information.

Local Network Usage: The Application utilizes local Wi-Fi / Local Area Network (LAN) connectivity solely to establish connection and manage communication between devices during a local multiplayer game session.

  • No data handled via the local network connection is ever transmitted outside of your local network.
  • No data is uploaded to any external servers, third parties, or the developer.

2.Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

No. The Application does not collect precise information about the location of your device. (Note: On some Android versions, scanning for local network / Wi-Fi configurations may trigger a system permission prompt, but the Application itself does not read, log, or store your location data.)

3.Third-party access

Since the Application does not collect or transmit any data, no third parties have access to your information through this Application.

4.Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

We do not collect or store any data, so there is no user data retained or managed by the developer. All game state, settings, and player setups are strictly stored locally on your own device and are cleared when the Application is closed or uninstalled.

5.Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here and updating the "Last updated" date above.
